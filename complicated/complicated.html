Frank's Trattoria
Established in 1980

Frank’s is one of my favourite places in new york, it's nothing like I imagined new york to be, and I thing thats the reason I loved it. Its a tiny, family-run Italian restaurant hidden behind an unassuming pizza shop. Inside, the cozy dining room feels like stepping into another era, where I’ve only ever seen older regulars who’ve likely been coming for years. The food tastes like something my grandmother would have made—simple, home-cooked, and full of love. It’s a reminder that amidst the ever-changing city, there are still pockets of history and genuine community untouched by big corporations.
