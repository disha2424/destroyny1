TRADER JOE'S
Food is essential for international students when familiar options are scarce. I had never cooked before, so my first grocery trip was quite an experience. Trader Joe’s surprised me with its friendly atmosphere and spacious aisles, where I filled my cart with frozen meals that helped me start my cooking journey and adapt to my new life.

Trader Joe’s feels like a slice of suburbia nestled in Manhattan, where friendly conversations are easy and the aisles are refreshingly spacious. It’s a place where the hustle of the city fades, allowing for a relaxed shopping experience. It was here that I gained the confidence to navigate my new independence, marking my first steps into adulthood. With each visit, I embraced responsibility and discovered a sense of belonging, making it a unique gem amidst the typical New York chaos. Even the prices seem different—more approachable and less overwhelming.
